US Economy

Get insights into the current state of the US economy.

General Careers

Slow Growth: The US economy is projected to grow at an average
rate of 1.9% per year from 2023 to 2033.

This is slower compared to the growth rates in the 1980s and 1990s.

Job Market:The job market is expected to add about 6.7 million jobs over the next decade. However, the growth rate of total employment is projected to be
slower than in the past decade.

Labor Force Participation:Increased labor force participation and immigration have added to the labor supply.
However, the aging population is expect6ed to slow down the overall growth of the labor force.

Unemployment and Job Stability:Unemployment rates are slightly rising, and there is a trend of slowing payroll growth. This indicates a more stable
but less dynamic job market compared to the immediate years after COVID-19.
